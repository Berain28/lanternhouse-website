Our Profile

We do not merely advise. We are architects of transformation.

Lanternhouse Services is a business consulting firm focused on integrity-based strategic solutions, sustainable transformation, and regulatory excellence — collaborating with global and local networks to deliver contextual insights.

01 — Who We Are
EST. JAKARTA · 2023

Since its establishment in Jakarta in 2023, Lanternhouse Services has pursued a vision to transform the business consulting landscape in Indonesia — combining global expertise with a profound understanding of local context.

In a time of significant change, we offer guidance on how to move forward. Our multidisciplinary team of strategists, data scientists, industry veterans, and sustainability experts partners with clients from start-ups to multinationals — delivering tailored solutions that drive growth through market-shaping strategies, resilience via risk-intelligent frameworks, and legacy by embedding purpose into business models.

The “Lantern” in our name is our commitment — to guide organisations through uncertainty with clarity, wisdom, and unwavering ethical conviction.
